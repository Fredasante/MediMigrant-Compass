About Us

Who We Are

MediMigrant Compass e.V. is a nonprofit organization founded in 2019 by Solace Yaa Gawu, a certified nurse and transcultural therapy specialist. Formerly known as MyHealth MyAfrica Team e.V., our mission has expanded to include migrants and changemakers from across the globe.

Why the Name Change?

As of 2024, our membership has become beautifully diverse—reflecting not only African heritage but also active engagement from individuals across South America, Madagascar, Eastern Europe, and other regions. To honor this evolution, we are transitioning from MyHealth MyAfrica Team e.V. to MediMigrant Compass e.V.—a name that better reflects our inclusive, global vision.

Our Vision is foster health, education, and social inclusion across borders—empowering individuals to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Programs

Our Work in Germany

Empowering Migrants Through Education & Integration. We support migrants—especially youth, families, and job seekers—as they build new lives in Germany.

Language & Integration Support

Courses and activities to improve language skills and promote successful integration into society.

Vocational & Academic Guidance

Helping migrants navigate educational and career paths with tailored advice and mentoring.

Internship Placement

Connecting migrants with internships in healthcare and social sectors to gain experience and skills.

Psychosocial Care & Homework Assistance

Offering support for emotional wellbeing and academic success for migrant families and youth.

Empowerment Workshops

Training sessions and mentorship programs to build confidence and leadership skills in migrants.

Public Symposia & Job-Search Congresses

Organizing forums that connect migrants with opportunities and share knowledge for success.

Awareness Campaigns

Educating communities on the dangers of irregular migration and promoting safe alternatives.

Community-Building Projects

Creating inclusive communities where migrants feel supported, valued, and connected.

Our Work in Africa

Strengthening Health & Awareness in Ghana and Nigeria. We collaborate with local communities and Community Health Workers to improve access to care and education.

Health Education

Providing awareness on preventable diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, kidney failure, and breast cancer to improve community wellbeing.

Mobile Outreach

Delivering health education and basic care to underserved rural communities through mobile health programs.

Medical Equipment Donations

Supplying local clinics and hospitals with essential medical equipment and materials to strengthen healthcare delivery.

Migration Risk Campaigns

Raising awareness of the dangers of irregular migration through educational campaigns in vulnerable communities.

Training Local Health Workers

Supporting and equipping Community Health Workers with skills and resources to provide sustainable healthcare services.

Who We Serve

Our Target Groups

We support diverse communities across Germany and Africa, empowering migrants, students, healthcare workers, and rural communities to access better healthcare, education, and opportunities.

Migrants in Germany

Supporting migrants seeking education, employment, and integration with tailored programs that provide guidance, resources, and community support.

Students & School Leavers

Offering mentorship and orientation programs for students and young people navigating educational pathways and career choices.

Job Seekers

Guiding job seekers, particularly those interested in healthcare careers, with training, networking, and employment opportunities.

Rural Communities in Africa

Providing essential medical support, health education, and resources to underserved rural communities in Ghana and Nigeria.

Volunteers & Professionals

Welcoming dedicated volunteers and healthcare professionals committed to advancing global health justice and community development.
