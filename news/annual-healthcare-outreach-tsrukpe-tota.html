Empowering Communities Through Health and Education - January 2024 Outreach in North Dayi

In January 2024, MediMigrant Compass e.V. Deutschland, in collaboration with the MyHealth MyAfrica Outreach Team - Ghana, organized its annual healthcare outreach in Tsrukpe-Tota, within the North Dayi Constituency. The initiative brought together traditional leaders, educators, and health professionals from nearby communities including Botoku, Anfoega, Vakpo, Brada Tornu, and the Awate Health Post. Schools across the area also took part, showcasing the region's strong commitment to community growth and development.

A major highlight of the program was the donation of school supplies to pupils in need â€” a gesture aimed at promoting education and inspiring lifelong learning. The event was warmly received by the community and emphasized the transformative power of investing in the next generation. In attendance were representatives from the Anfoega District Education Directorate, the District Chief Executive, Hon. Kudjo Attah, and teachers from various schools, all united in advancing health and education as foundations for sustainable progress.
